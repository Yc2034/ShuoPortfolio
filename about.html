HELLO,I AM SHUO HAO

From a young age, my passion for painting was evident. I began formal training at the age of 7, a pursuit that eventually led me to the Tianjin Academy of Fine Arts, where I earned a Bachelor's degree in Fashion and Textile Design. After graduation, I immersed myself in the fashion world, accumulating over seven years of professional experience. Recognizing the growing prominence of digital media, I expanded my skills by undertaking a program in Interactive Media Management. This course ignited a new passion within me for digital design, encompassing areas like graphic design, UX design, website creation, animation, and video production. I envision these fields being the cornerstone of my future design career.

HOBBIES AND INTERESTS

Painting

Painting serves as a therapeutic escape for me. Not only does it hone my critical thinking, but it also sharpens my fine motor skills. Through each brush stroke, I build confidence, cultivate positivity, and foster emotional development.
